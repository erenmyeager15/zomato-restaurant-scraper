# Zomato Restaurant Scraper Promotion Notes

## Positioning

Collect structured public Zomato restaurant listings for restaurant research, cuisine analysis, and local market intelligence.

## Best small demo

- City: `Mumbai`
- Search: `pizza`
- Minimum rating: `0`
- Maximum results: `1`
- Proxy: Apify Residential, country `IN`

Show the input, one clean dataset row, and JSON or CSV export. Keep the demo factual and short.

## LinkedIn draft

I published a Zomato Restaurant Scraper on Apify for structured restaurant market research.

It collects public listing fields such as restaurant name, cuisines, rating, review count, cost for two, locality, address snippet, image URL, and restaurant URL.

The safest first run is intentionally small: Mumbai, pizza, one result, and an India residential proxy. Inspect the output, then scale the result limit or add a rating filter.

Useful for cuisine research, local competitor analysis, and restaurant BI datasets.
